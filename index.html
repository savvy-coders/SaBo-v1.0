What is Sabo?

SaBo is a friendly, code-savvy teaching assistant

SaBo - short for Savvy Bot - is designed to help students along the prep and onboarding process.

What is SaBo good for?


Current features: 

- Gives summaries on curriculum information
- Expands on curriculum content to reword and help reinforce content

Future Iterations will include:
- the ability to submit assignments and receive feedback
- grade assignments and send results to staff or systems
- answer questions live for applicants on the Savvy Website
- Pass off to humans for complicated questions
- Update Jira tickets and github merge requests
